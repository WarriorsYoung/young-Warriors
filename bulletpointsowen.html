Does this feel like you?

Many people begin their journey feeling: unworthy, overwhelmed or lost. Stuck in limiting beliefs and old patterns. Disconnected from their purpose, unsure of where to turn. Controlled by external circumstances. Struggling to break free.
At 11 years old I felt this too. My life took a big turn when my brother, my rock, got into legal trouble. A void opened that led to depression, feelings of abandonment and disempowerment. Seeking support from my parents only deepened the pain. My dad left without telling me, and my parents divorced, leaving me feeling completely abandoned. I turned to addictions to fill the void. Not realizing the answers I sought were within me.
