“Your ‘Why’ is the Seed to Your Tree of Life”

Do you ever feel like you’re chasing goals that don’t truly fulfill you? Or that you’re working hard but still feeling empty inside? Here’s the truth: your life’s purpose isn’t about what you want—it’s about why you want it. Every dream or goal you have is like a seed. But without clarity on your “why,” that seed can’t grow. To water it, you need to understand the emotions and meaning behind your desires.

Ask yourself:

  • Why do I want this?
  • What do I believe this will make me feel?
  • Am I aligned with the deeper meaning behind this desire?

Finding your “why” isn’t about adding something external to your life—it’s about rediscovering the truth that’s already inside you. And once you uncover that truth, you’ll begin to radiate it outward naturally, impacting every area of your life.
